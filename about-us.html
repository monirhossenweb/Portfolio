Our Story

JHON MARK
Principal Physiotherapist

John started his career as a Physiotherapist at Tan Tock Seng Hospital as a neuro physiotherapist, helping patients with stroke, Parkinson’s disease and spinal cord injuries. He has attained a Degree with Honours in Physiotherapy

In the course of his work, John uses The Bobath Concept and Neuro-facilitation to improve his clients mobility and quality of life.His clients ranged from 15 years old with spinal cord issues to 92 years old elderly with parkinson’s disease. He believes everyone has a potential for improvement and deserves a shot at improving their quality of life.

John is trained in the following concepts:-Stroke Rehabilitation-Parkinson’s Disease Rehabilitation-Spinal Cord Injury Rehabilitation-Balance and Gait RehabilitationHe holds the following certifications:-Bobath Concept-Neuro-facilitation-Selective Functional Movement Assessment

His skillset allows his to safely and efficiently identify neurological issues such as weakness, spasticity, balance and gait disturbances and improve his clients functional levels.
